IDEA #1 (DEVIN)
Issue: Accessible ML tool for people w/o ML background
Target Audience: Business Analyst, Data Analyst, Domain Experts

A web application that consist of the following components:
  1. ML Store (like an appstore but for ML models) - place where people can upload and download trained ML models
  2. ML Lab - place where people can create and train their own ML models as well as deploy/test other people ML models from the ML Store
  3. Dashboard tools - place where people can analyze data and generate whatever statistics/graphs they want (mainly for data analytics)
